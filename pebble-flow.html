Silent HVAC

Whether you want to heat things up or cool things off, simply pre-set the whisper-quiet HVAC with the Apt 24® App so it’s comfortable as soon as you set up camp. The fully-insulated shell keeps the temperatures just right and the external ultra-high efficiency heat pump keeps things quiet.

FAQs
Do I need an EV to tow the Apt 24® Flow?

You don’t need an EV to tow The Apt 24® Flow.  Apt 24® Flow’s dual-motor Active Propulsion Assist System provides a safe and effortless towing experience and works great for gas, hybrid or EVs. As long as your vehicle has a towing capacity of 6,200 lbs, you’ll be able to tow the Apt 24® Flow.

How do I charge the Apt 24® Flow?

The Apt 24® Flow can be charged via  AC/DC charging at public stations, at home, campgrounds, and more. The Apt 24® Flow’s 1kW integrated solar self-charges on the go and is always topping off its power supply.

Does the Apt 24® App require the Internet to connect and communicate with the Apt 24® Flow?

No. All communication necessary for Apt 24® Flow's functions is kept within a local network, bypassing the need for cloud or internet service providers. This localized communication enables Apt 24® Flow's features to operate seamlessly, regardless of your location, even in remote areas.

What's included in the Magic Pack

The Magic Pack adds a powerful dual-motor drivetrain and bundles a set of world-first features to the Apt 24® Flow, including Remote Control, Magic Hitch, Easy Tow and more.

Can I power my home with the Apt 24® Flow?

Yes. The total battery capacity of the Apt 24® Flow is 3.5x that of a Tesla Powerwall, making it a great home backup battery in the case of an emergency.
